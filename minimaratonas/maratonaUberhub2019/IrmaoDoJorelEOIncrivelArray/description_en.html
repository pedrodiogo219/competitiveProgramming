Jorel's Brother And The Amazing Array

By Gustavo Candido de Freitas Simões, Universidade Federal de Uberlândia Brazil

Timelimit: 1

The author of this problem is a big fan of Jorel’s brother and always visits him to play. However, in his last visit he found his friend with problems. Apparently Jorel’s brother got an amazing array A from his sweet grandma that consists of N positive integers and he developed a manner to transform it in a table T where Tᵢⱼ represents the multiplication of elements Aᵢ and Aⱼ from the original array. Jorel’s brother said that with his transformation technique he will finally recognized and never will be called only “Jorel’s brother” again. But he can’t remember exactly how was his original array, so he can’t use his technique anymore.

The author, knowing your abilities as a programmer, asks you to help Jorel’s brother recuperate his amazing array.

 

Input

The first line of the input contains an integer N (0<N<10³) and then N*N numbers representing the table T​ (0< Tᵢⱼ <10⁹)

Output

The output is N numbers separate by one empty space representing the original array
